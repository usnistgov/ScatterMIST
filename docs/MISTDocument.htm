5. MIST Document

The MIST Document format is the preferred way to provide instructions for MIST to determine the integrated reflectance. A new document may be opened by selecting the menu item File$\rightarrow$New. A saved document may be opened by selecting File$\rightarrow$Open. A saved MIST Document will contain not only the instructions to generate the integrated reflectance, but also data from the last calculation.
